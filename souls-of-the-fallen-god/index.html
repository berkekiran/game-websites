Story


God Eirwen sent 3 different meteors to earth in ancient times. One of these meteorites fell into the dark lands, the other into the bright lands, and the other into the green lands where the humans are. These meteorites contained power crystals. However, only the strongest beings could use these crystals.

Rudite, the third of the greatest of the lords of men, gave these crystals to Bargeraz, one of the lords of the dark realms, to strengthen his own kingdom. Bargeraz, on the other hand, use these crystals to bring death to all their kingdoms in the green realm.

While all this was going on, Alvis was living a quiet life alone in the forests of Issar. But death had come to the forests of Issar, leaving all its enemies behind. Would Alvis open the box containing the shattered magical crystal left behind by his great-grandfather, who had remained untouched for generations?
